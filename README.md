# GlobeBudget 🌍💰

## Overview

GlobeBudget is a Product Management and UX Case Study focused on helping international students manage expenses, budgets, emergency savings, and multiple currencies through a simple mobile-first fintech application.

---

## Problem Statement

International students often struggle with:

* Expense Tracking
* Budget Management
* Currency Conversion
* Emergency Savings Planning

Most finance applications are designed for general users and do not address the specific challenges faced by international students.

---

## Solution

GlobeBudget provides:

* Expense Tracking
* Budget Planning
* Currency Converter
* Emergency Fund Monitoring
* Spending Analytics
